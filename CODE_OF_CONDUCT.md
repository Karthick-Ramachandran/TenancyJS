# Code of Conduct

TenancyJS contributors must communicate professionally, evaluate technical claims on evidence, and
make the project safe for people of different backgrounds and experience levels.

Harassment, discrimination, threats, sexualized attention, deliberate intimidation, publishing
private information, and sustained disruption are not acceptable. Maintainers may edit or remove
content, reject contributions, or restrict participation to protect the community.

Report conduct concerns privately to the maintainers through the repository's private contact or
security-reporting channel. Reports will be handled as confidentially as practical.
